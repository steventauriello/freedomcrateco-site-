Caliber Collection

Handcrafted wooden ammo boxes sized for your favorite calibers — 9mm, 5.56, and 7.62 / .308 — with your choice of black or stainless hardware and optional Armor Core interior liner. Built in Florida, built for real use at the range.

Specifications

  • Interior dimensions: 11″ W × 8¼″ L × 5½″ D (fits loose rounds, boxed ammo, and mags)
  • Exterior dimensions: Approx. 14″ W × 9½″ L × 7″ H
  • Construction: ¾″ exterior-grade plywood walls with reinforced legs and lid rails
  • Bottom panel: ¼″ sanded plywood, glued and screwed for strength
  • Lid: Reinforced solid-wood top with support rails for stacking
  • Finish: Ultra-flat service green (range-ready, low-glare finish)
  • Hardware: Your choice of black steel or stainless steel lockable toggle latches
  • Hinges: Stainless steel rear hinges
  • Handles: Heavy-duty nylon webbing, routed through the legs so it can’t pull out
  • Calibers sized for: 9mm, 5.56, and 7.62 / .308
  • Interior liner: Standard wood or optional Armor Core interior PolyShield for extra protection.

Built to work at the range

  • Leg design keeps the box up off wet ground and protects the lid when stacking.
  • Reinforced lid rails give you a solid “bench” surface for mags, tools, or a notebook.
  • Handles are integrated into the legs so the webbing can’t wiggle loose or rip out over time.
  • Armor Core interior option resists oil and solvent, making wipe-downs quick and easy.
  • Each box is sprayed, stenciled, and finished by hand in Florida.
  • Free U.S. shipping; some orders may ship in two packages (both tracking numbers provided).
