About Freedom Crate Co.

"Built for Patriots. Inspired by History. Crafted by Hand."

Our Story

Freedom Crate Co. was born out of a simple idea: everyday Americans deserve products that carry meaning, strength, and history. What started as one handcrafted ammo box has grown into a collection of patriotic crates — each one designed to honor our military heritage, our freedoms, and the craftsmanship that built this country.

What Makes Us Different

  • Veteran-inspired craftsmanship: Every crate is built with precision and respect for America’s armed forces.
  • Authenticity: Designs are based on real Cold War–era ammo boxes, made with solid wood, steel hardware, and stenciling.
  • Made in Florida: Each Freedom Crate is built and shipped by hand in the U.S.A.
  • For Patriots, by Patriots: Whether you’re a veteran, collector, or simply someone who loves this country — these crates are built for you.

Our Mission

To build more than just boxes — to create lasting symbols of freedom, strength, and American heritage. Every crate tells a story, and every order helps keep American craftsmanship alive.
