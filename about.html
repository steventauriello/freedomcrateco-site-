About Freedom Crate Co.

Freedom Crate Co. was built on the belief that American craftsmanship should stand the test of time. Every crate is handcrafted in Florida with pride, precision, and respect for the men and women who serve our nation.

Our ammo boxes are inspired by authentic Cold War–era designs, reimagined with modern durability and personalization options. Whether for storage, display, or a meaningful gift, each crate tells a story of patriotism and tradition.
