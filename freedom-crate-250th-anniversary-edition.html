Commemorating America’s 250th Birthday

Celebrating 250 Years of American Independence, the Freedom Crate™ 250th Anniversary Edition is a limited-production handcrafted crate built to honor America’s 250th Birthday. Built tough and finished with pride, this edition is both functional and collectible, featuring a distressed American flag, EST. 1776 markings, “Freedom Is Not Free” graphics, and military-inspired styling throughout.

Whether used for tools, keepsakes, collectibles, documents, range gear, or personal items, this crate is designed to serve as durable storage and a meaningful tribute to America’s heritage.

Limited Production Through 2026

To celebrate America’s 250th anniversary, this edition will be produced through the remainder of 2026. Once the anniversary year concludes, this edition will be retired from production.

Armor Core™ Interior

This edition includes Freedom Crate Co.’s Armor Core™ Interior — a deep-penetrating epoxy treatment that seals and fortifies the inside of the box. This protective layer hardens within the wood fibers, creating a rigid, moisture-resistant shell that helps shield against humidity, oil, and impact while preserving the handcrafted character of each crate.

Hidden Under the Lid

Beneath the lid is the Latin phrase “Non Sibi Sed Patriae”, meaning “Not for Self, but for Country.” For generations, this motto has represented service, sacrifice, and devotion to something greater than oneself.
