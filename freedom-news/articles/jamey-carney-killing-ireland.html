American Mother Found Dead in Ireland as International Search Continues for Boyfriend

Irish authorities have identified the victim's boyfriend as the primary suspect after he allegedly fled the country before her body was discovered.

By Steven Tauriello • Freedom News • July 11, 2026

Freedom News Report: This article is based on publicly available reporting and official statements released by Irish authorities. Allegations against any suspect remain allegations unless and until they are proven in court.

Irish authorities are continuing an international search after 43-year-old American mother Jamey Carney was found dead inside her home in Killarney, County Kerry, Ireland.

Police have identified Ahmad Al-Saqar, Carney's boyfriend, as the primary suspect after investigators determined he allegedly left Ireland before her body was discovered.

According to Irish investigators, Carney's body was discovered inside her residence by her thirteen-year-old daughter. The Irish State Pathologist determined that Carney died from suffocation following an assault.

Authorities have not publicly released additional forensic details regarding the precise mechanism of her death.

Investigators believe Al-Saqar traveled from County Kerry to Dublin before boarding a flight to Istanbul, Turkey. Irish police are now working alongside international law-enforcement agencies as efforts continue to locate him.

A Relationship Shared Publicly

The case has attracted international attention because Carney and Al-Saqar frequently appeared together on social media. Public posts showed the couple attending demonstrations in Ireland and sharing photographs documenting their relationship.

Carney was publicly involved in humanitarian and pro-Palestinian advocacy. Her activism and her relationship with the man now being sought by investigators have become part of the wider public discussion surrounding the case.

An Investigation Crossing International Borders

Authorities believe the suspect departed Ireland shortly after Carney's death, transforming what began as a local homicide investigation into an international search.

Irish investigators are coordinating with foreign authorities as they attempt to determine the suspect's current whereabouts and pursue the appropriate legal process.

Police have appealed to the public for information regarding the suspect's movements and continue investigating the circumstances leading up to Carney's death.

Questions Beyond the Investigation

The case has generated public discussion well beyond Ireland. Much of that discussion has focused on Carney's activism, her relationship with the suspect, immigration policy, integration and public safety across Europe.

As with any developing criminal investigation, verified facts should be distinguished from speculation circulating across social media.

For Carney's family, the political debate remains secondary to a much more personal tragedy. A mother has lost her life, a young daughter has lost her parent, and investigators are continuing their search for the man they believe may have answers about what happened.
