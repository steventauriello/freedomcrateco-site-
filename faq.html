Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about our wooden ammo boxes, range crates, and ordering.

Where can I buy a wooden military ammo box online?

You’re already in the right place. All of our handcrafted wooden military-style ammo boxes are sold directly through FreedomCrateCo.com. We build every box here in Florida and ship anywhere in the United States with free U.S. shipping.

Are your wooden ammo boxes actually usable at the range?

Yes. Our ammo boxes and range crates are built to be used, not just displayed. We use exterior-grade plywood, rugged hardware, and durable finishes so customers can load them up for live range days, vehicle storage, and long-term home organization.

Can I get custom stenciling or a specific caliber?

Absolutely. We can stencil branches, slogans, or calibers and also build one-of-one boxes on request. Use the Custom Orders form to tell us what you’re looking for and we’ll get back to you with options and pricing.

Do you support veterans with your business?

Freedom Crate Co. is veteran-inspired and Florida-built. A portion of our efforts goes toward The Final Salute Project, which focuses on honoring recently retired U.S. veterans with handcrafted keepsake boxes.

How long does shipping take and is it really free?

Yes, shipping is free within the United States. Most orders ship within a few days and typically arrive in about 3–7 business days, depending on your location and carrier timing.

Do you offer local pickup?

At this time we primarily ship orders, but if you are in Florida and interested in local pickup, reach out through the Contact / Custom Orders page and we’ll let you know if it’s possible for your order.

What if there’s an issue with my order?

If something arrives damaged or not quite right, contact us as soon as possible with your order number and photos. We’ll work with you to make it right and get you a box you’re proud to own or give as a gift.

Will my box look exactly like the photos?

Because each Freedom Crate Co. box is built in small batches, certain details such as wood grain, paint finish, stenciling placement, and hardware components may vary slightly from the product photos shown.

Depending on sourcing and availability, equivalent or upgraded handles, hinges, latches, or fasteners may be used while maintaining the intended durability, functionality, and overall design of the crate.

What is your return policy?

Standard, non-custom products (anything that is not a one-of-one or custom-made build) can be returned within 14 days of delivery as long as they are unused and in original condition. The buyer is responsible for return shipping costs.

Custom-made orders and one-of-one builds are non-returnable and non-refundable, since they’re built specifically for you. If a custom box arrives damaged or with a workmanship issue, reach out to us and we’ll review it on a case-by-case basis to make it right.
