Handcrafted Wood Ammo Boxes

Our Caliber Collection is built for shooters looking for durable, handcrafted wood ammo boxes with a classic military-inspired look. Whether you need a wooden ammo box for range use, ammo storage, or display, these crates are built to be used and built to last.

Each wood ammo box is made in Florida and designed to give you a more rugged, distinctive alternative to ordinary plastic or metal ammo storage.

Handcrafted ammo crates with a mission

Each Caliber Collection crate is built one at a time in Florida by Freedom Crate Co. — routed, assembled, sprayed, and stenciled by hand. The design is inspired by classic military surplus crates, with caliber-specific markings (for example, “9mm Ball M882 – 1000 RDS”) and lot numbers for authenticity and tracking.

A portion of every Caliber Collection sale helps fund The Final Salute Project, our effort to gift custom keepsake boxes to recently retired U.S. veterans. When you pick up one of these crates, you’re not just buying storage — you’re helping honor someone’s service.

Armor Core™ interior (optional upgrade)

Choose the optional Armor Core™ Interior for maximum protection. This is a deep-penetrating epoxy treatment that soaks into the grain and hardens inside the wood fibers, creating a rigid, moisture-resistant shell on the inside of the crate. It’s designed to stand up to:

  • Humidity and moisture changes
  • Oil, carbon, and cleaning solvent from range gear
  • Everyday dings, drops, and impact

Armor Core is our toughest interior system so far — built for people who actually use their gear.

Dimensions

  • Exterior: Approx. 14" L × 11" W × 9" H
  • Interior storage: Approx. 11" L × 9" W × 5 7/8" H
  • Calibers sized for: 9mm, 5.56 / .223, and 7.62 / .308 (box size is shared; markings are caliber-specific)

Construction & hardware

  • Material: 3/4" exterior-grade plywood — strong but lighter than solid timber for easier handling
  • Finish: Flat olive drab / service green, hand-sprayed for a rugged, low-glare look
  • Stencils: Caliber-specific markings (e.g., “9mm Ball M882 · 1000 RDS”) applied in small batches
  • Lot number: Freedom Crate Co. lot marking for authenticity (varies by batch)
  • Lid: Reinforced slat-style lid with raised rails that double as a small staging surface
  • Bottom panel: 1/4" sanded plywood, glued and screwed into a full perimeter frame
  • Legs: Stackable support legs are secured using eight 1.25" SPAX® screws (two per leg) for exceptional holding power and long-term durability. The legs are designed to be just slightly taller than the lid, so when stacked, the weight of the crate above rests on the legs — not the lid.
  • Screw-built construction: SPAX® fasteners are also used throughout the crate body to reduce racking and increase structural strength — where true surplus crates traditionally use nails, we upgrade to screws for superior longevity and holding power.
  • Handles: Heavy steel D-ring folding side handles for confident carry
  • Hardware options: Black steel or stainless steel lockable toggle latches
  • Hinges: Stainless tri-fold rear hinges
  • Interior: Raw wood or Armor Core™ epoxy-sealed interior (no interior corner brackets on this model)
