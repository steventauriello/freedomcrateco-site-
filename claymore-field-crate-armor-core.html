M18A1-Inspired Field Crate Design

Inspired by the iconic M18A1 Claymore mine crate, this handcrafted wooden storage box is a faithful military-style reproduction designed for storage, display, and long-term durability. Built to capture the unmistakable look of a field-issued container, it features authentic stenciling and rugged construction true to historic military packaging.

This crate is intended for storage, organization, or display purposes only. It is a handcrafted homage to historic military packaging and is not a functional weapon container.

Armor Core™ Interior Protection

This Armor Core Edition is fully lined with Freedom Crate Co.’s proprietary Armor Core™ epoxy-reinforced interior coating. The lining is designed to improve durability, add moisture resistance, and protect the inside of the crate against oil, wear, and long-term use while preserving the classic exterior silhouette.

Slide-Top Military Crate Construction

Constructed from 3/4-inch UTX exterior-grade plywood and finished in true military Olive Drab, this crate delivers historical realism with enhanced modern durability. The traditional slide-top lid keeps the field-crate look while allowing fast access to the interior storage compartment.
