M18A1-Inspired Field Crate Design

Inspired by the iconic M18A1 Claymore mine crate, this handcrafted wooden storage box is a faithful military-style reproduction designed for storage, display, and long-term durability. Built to capture the unmistakable look of a field-issued container, it features authentic stenciling and rugged construction true to historic military packaging.

This crate is intended for storage, organization, or display purposes only. It is a handcrafted homage to historic military packaging and is not a functional weapon container.

Standard Edition Interior

This Standard Edition features a natural unfinished wood interior that keeps the crate rugged, simple, and true to its handcrafted field-crate character. It is ideal for range gear, tools, keepsakes, collectibles, workshop organization, and display use.

Slide-Top Military Crate Construction

Constructed from 3/4-inch UTX exterior-grade plywood and finished in true military Olive Drab, this crate delivers historical realism with modern durability. The traditional slide-top lid keeps the field-crate look while allowing fast access to the interior storage compartment.
