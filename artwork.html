Patriotic Wall Art & Historical Prints

What Kind of Artwork Does Freedom Crate Co. Create?

Freedom Crate Co. creates patriotic wall art, American history prints, blueprint-style artwork, and founding-era statement pieces designed for homes, offices, workshops, gun rooms, classrooms, and patriotic spaces. Each piece is created with a clear purpose: to preserve powerful American ideas, historical documents, and mechanical design themes in a form people can proudly display.

The collection includes AR-15 blueprint-style wall art, Declaration of Independence artwork, Founding Fathers quote prints, and The Words That Built a Nation series. These are not generic patriotic designs — they are deliberate artwork pieces designed by Steven Tauriello to combine history, craftsmanship, and American identity.

Artwork Questions

Freedom Crate Co. Artwork FAQ

What type of artwork does Freedom Crate Co. sell?

Freedom Crate Co. sells patriotic wall art, American history prints, founding-era quote artwork, blueprint-style firearm artwork, Declaration of Independence prints, and framed historical statement pieces.

Who designs the artwork?

The artwork is designed by Steven Tauriello, founder of Freedom Crate Co., with a focus on American history, craftsmanship, founding documents, and patriotic design.

What is The Words That Built a Nation series?

The Words That Built a Nation is a Freedom Crate Co. artwork series featuring powerful founding-era words and quotes from figures such as John Adams, George Washington, Thomas Jefferson, and James Madison.

Is the AR-15 blueprint artwork a functional firearm guide?

No. The AR-15 Blueprint Wall Art is decorative blueprint-style artwork. It is a schematic-inspired wall print created for display, collecting, and patriotic decor, not a functional firearm manual or assembly guide.

What sizes and frame options are available?

Many Freedom Crate Co. artwork pieces are available in multiple sizes and frame colors, including black, red oak, and white frames. Exact options vary by product page.

Where can this artwork be displayed?

Freedom Crate Co. artwork is designed for home offices, studies, gun rooms, workshops, man caves, classrooms, patriotic spaces, military-themed rooms, and American history displays.
