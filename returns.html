Return Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

1. Our Approach

At Freedom Crate Co., our products are handcrafted and built with care. We want customers to feel confident ordering from us, while also protecting the integrity of custom and made-to-order work.

This Return Policy explains which items may be returned, which items are final sale, and how refunds are handled.

2. Return Window

Eligible items may be returned within 14 days of delivery.

To be eligible for a return, the item must be:

  • Unused
  • In its original condition
  • Returned in appropriate protective packaging

3. Return Shipping

The buyer is responsible for return shipping costs unless the item arrived damaged or there was an error with the order.

Original shipping costs, if any, are non-refundable unless otherwise required by law.

4. Non-Returnable Items

Because many Freedom Crate Co. items are made to order or customized, some items are not eligible for return.

  • Custom orders are final sale
  • Items with personalized markings, custom stenciling, or custom modifications are not returnable
  • Any item returned used, altered, or damaged after delivery may be denied a refund

5. Damaged or Incorrect Orders

If your order arrives damaged, or if you receive the wrong item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery.

Please include:

  • Your order details
  • A brief description of the issue
  • Clear photos of the item and packaging

We will review the issue and work with you to make it right.

6. Refunds

Once a returned item is received and inspected, any approved refund will be issued to the original payment method.

If the returned item does not meet the conditions of this policy, the refund may be reduced or denied.

7. Exchanges

We do not guarantee direct exchanges on all items. If there is a problem with your order, please contact us first so we can review the best solution.

8. How to Start a Return

To request a return, please contact us through the Contact page and include your order details and reason for the return.

Please do not send an item back without contacting us first.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Return Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

This Return Policy is provided for informational purposes and does not constitute legal advice. For specific legal guidance, please consult a qualified attorney.
