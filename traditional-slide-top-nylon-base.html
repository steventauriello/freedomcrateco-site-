Classic Slide Top Design

The Traditional Slide Top Ammo Crate was designed to capture the look and simplicity of early wooden military shipping crates. Rather than using hinges, toggle latches, or other lid hardware, the removable top can either be lifted straight off or slid off from the front or rear of the crate.

The result is a cleaner, more traditional appearance with fewer moving parts. It is simple, rugged, and true to the wooden field crates that inspired it.

  • No front latch hardware
  • No rear hinge hardware
  • Removable slide-top lid
  • Lid can be lifted off or slid off from the front or rear
  • Classic military-inspired appearance

The Engineering Behind the Crate

While the design looks simple, considerable thought went into how the lid is retained. Unlike many slide-top boxes, there are no routed grooves or slide rails built into the crate itself.

Instead, the corner support legs serve a dual purpose. In addition to protecting the bottom of the crate and allowing multiple crates to stack securely, they also prevent the lid from shifting side to side during normal use. This keeps the lid properly aligned while still allowing it to be removed easily whenever access is needed.
