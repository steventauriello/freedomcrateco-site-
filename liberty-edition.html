Built for Storage. Designed as Functional Americana.

Built tough and finished with pride, the Liberty Edition blends Revolutionary War heritage with rugged military styling. The front features a hand-applied blacked-out Gadsden Flag surrounded by the iconic 13 Betsy Ross stars, creating a clean, dark patriotic look that stands apart from standard ammo crates.

The historic Philadelphia coordinates are stenciled onto one leg, while the opposing leg features the date 07-04-1776, paying tribute to the birth of American independence.
